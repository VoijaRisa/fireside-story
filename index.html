It was a dark and stormy night...

The young page startled from sleep as an armor stand crashed over.

This is why I shouldn't let the damn cat in my room at night, he thought to himself.

Nervously he tossed back the blankets and shivered as the chilly air hit his flimsy shift.

He stepped from the bed. As his feet hit the cold stone floor a hand snaked out from beneath the bed.

It seized his ankle, pulling his legs from beneath him.
